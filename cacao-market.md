<p><p>The cacao market is primarily divided into two segments: cacao particles and cacao powder. Cacao powder, derived from grinding roasted cacao beans, is used in baking, beverages, and confections due to its concentrated chocolate flavor. Both market types cater to the growing demand for natural, high-quality ingredients in wellness-oriented and gourmet food products.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Three-Piece Cans</li><li>Two-Piece Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum beverage cans market primarily comprises two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate pieces—top, body, and bottom—typically welded or glued together, often used for larger beverages. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single aluminum sheet, forming the body and bottom seamlessly, offering a lightweight and aesthetically pleasing design. The two-piece design is increasingly popular due to its manufacturing efficiency and reduced material usage, making it the preferred choice for many beverage producers.</p></p>

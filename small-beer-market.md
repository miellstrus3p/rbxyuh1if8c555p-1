<p><p>The Small Beer Market encompasses two key segments: Limit Fermentation and Dealcoholization Method. Conversely, the Dealcoholization Method removes alcohol from regular beer, maintaining its original flavors while catering to those preferring non-alcoholic options. Both types meet the growing demand for lighter, sessionable drinks without compromising on taste or experience.</p></p>
